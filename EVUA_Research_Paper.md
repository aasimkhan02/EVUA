## Abstract
In this era of digital transformation, user authentication is a critical aspect of ensuring security across platforms. This research paper presents EVUA (Enhanced Virtual User Authentication), a novel approach to enhance the security framework of user authentication processes. We explore various authentication methods, their advantages, and weaknesses, ultimately proposing a hybrid model that incorporates biometric verification with traditional authentication techniques, aimed at increasing the reliability and security of these systems.
